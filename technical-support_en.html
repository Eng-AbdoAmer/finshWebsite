When you submit a support ticket for a car trip, it is important to provide detailed information about the issue or concern you are having.

Trip Details

State the date and time of the trip, the car rental company or service used, and any booking or reservation numbers associated with the trip..

Description of the problem

Clearly explain the problem you encountered during the trip. For example, if the car broke down, provide details of the symptoms, where they occurred, and any relevant circumstances.

Supporting Evidence
